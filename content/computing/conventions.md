# Greg's Conventions

* Parens or Parenthesis: `()`
* Left-Parens: `(`
* Right-Parens: `)`

* Brackets or Square Brackets: `[]`
* Left-Bracket: `[`
* Right-Bracket: `]`

* Braces or Curly Braces: `{}`
* Left-Curly-Brace: `{`
* Right-Curly-Brace: `}`
